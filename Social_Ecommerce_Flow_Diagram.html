How Commission Works — Example
A follower buys a ₹2,000 jacket through a creator's affiliate link. The brand has set a 15% commission rate (₹300). The platform keeps 20% of the commission (₹60). The creator receives 80% (₹240). The brand acquired a customer for ₹300 — far cheaper than a Facebook ad.

Critical: Tracking Must Be Bulletproof
If a creator's sale doesn't get tracked and attributed correctly, they lose income and trust in your platform immediately. Invest in robust affiliate tracking infrastructure from Day 1 — this is non-negotiable.
🎯
Why a Tiered System Instead of One Threshold
A single "100 followers = eligible" rule is too blunt. Creators need progressive incentives to stay engaged. Brands need confidence in who they're working with. A tiered system solves both problems and creates a natural growth path on your platform.

⚠️
Honest Note on Brand Deals at 100 Followers
Research shows only 9% of creators are willing to work on commission-only campaigns. At 100 followers, set expectations clearly: creators unlock the affiliate system and product gifting — not paid sponsorships. Paid brand deals realistically start at 1,000–10,000 followers depending on niche. Consider a small "new creator bonus" (₹100–500/month) to reduce early churn.
Platform Revenue Streams
💰
Commission Cut
20% of every affiliate commission paid through the platform. Scales automatically with Gross Merchandise Value (GMV). No cap — the more creators sell, the more you earn.
Core Revenue Stream · Scales with GMV
🏢
Brand Subscription Fee
Monthly SaaS fee for brands to access the Creator Marketplace, post collab briefs, and view creator analytics. Tiered by number of active campaigns and brand size.
₹5,000 – ₹50,000 / month per brand
🔝
Featured Placement
Brands pay to boost their collab brief or product listing to the top of the creator discovery feed. Pay-per-boost or monthly featured slot.
₹2,000 – ₹20,000 per campaign
⭐
Creator Pro Tier (V2)
Premium subscription for creators: advanced analytics, benchmark comparisons, early access to brand campaigns, priority brand matching, and dedicated support.
₹299 – ₹999 / month per creator
💳
Payout Processing Fee (V2)
Small fee charged when creators withdraw earnings through the platform wallet. Applied only on successful payouts, making it low-friction for creators.
1 – 2% of payout value
⚠️
What NOT to Monetize Early
Don't charge creators to join. Don't take too large a commission cut (above 25% will cause creator churn). Don't paywall basic analytics — creators need to see their earnings to trust you.
Avoid these in V1
